Healthcare MIS & Business Intelligence

Managing Healthcare MIS Reporting, Insurance Analytics, Revenue Cycle Monitoring, and Reporting Automation for multi-location hospital operations.

Dispatch Report Management

  • Download and consolidate dispatch reports from 5 hospital locations using HMS (Hospital Management System).
  • Manage and analyze historical patient data from 2019 to present covering lakhs of patient records.
  • Generate consolidated MIS reports for top management to monitor insurance claims and billing status.
  • Monitor billing submissions for CGHS, ESIC, ECHS, CAPF, and TPA insurance schemes.
  • Use Python scripts and AI tools to automate repetitive reporting and workflow processes.

Unbilled Revenue – Patient Wise Analysis

  • Prepare reports identifying admitted patients whose final bills are not yet generated.
  • Track provisional billing versus advance payments to identify outstanding balances.
  • Monitor revenue risk and ensure advance collections align with interim billing amounts.
  • Example: Provisional Bill ₹10 | Advance Received ₹8 | Outstanding ₹2

Patient Pending Bills Monitoring

  • Generate reports for patients with outstanding balances after bill generation.
  • Assist finance teams in tracking overdue payments and improving revenue collection efficiency.
  • Support revenue cycle monitoring and payment follow-up operations.

Billing Generated but Payment Pending

  • Identify cases where bills are generated but payment is pending from patients or insurers.
  • Track pending payment cases for operational and revenue cycle management.
  • Prepare payment status reports for management review.

Authorization Discount Monitoring

  • Generate reports for patient bills pending discount approval from authorized personnel.
  • Monitor billing delays caused by pending approvals.
  • Ensure timely coordination for billing closure and approval processing.

Insurance Query Management (NMI Reports)

  • Handle ECHS insurance query reports and coordinate with departments for claim resolution.
  • Track and manage ESIC insurance claim queries and clarification reports.
  • Manage TMS NMI OTP query reports and internal coordination workflows.
  • Prepare consolidated MIS reports for insurance query tracking.
